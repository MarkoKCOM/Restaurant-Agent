## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: General membership FAQ exists for reuse
OpenSeat SHALL provide a general membership FAQ that is reusable across restaurants.

#### Scenario: Operator needs a reusable answer
- WHEN an operator or support owner needs to answer a membership question
- THEN OpenSeat SHALL provide a general FAQ covering points, rewards, claims, referrals, and message preferences
- AND the FAQ SHALL NOT depend on one specific restaurant brand or pilot location

### Requirement: Operators have a practical membership playbook
OpenSeat SHALL provide an operator-facing guide for running loyalty responsibly.

#### Scenario: Owner launches or reviews the club
- WHEN an owner/admin needs to run loyalty day to day
- THEN OpenSeat SHALL provide guidance for reward strategy, referral visibility, redemption flow, and consent handling
- AND that guidance SHALL be suitable for reuse across restaurants
