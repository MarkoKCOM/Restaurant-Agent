## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Owner/admin membership operations
Restaurant admins SHALL be able to configure and operate the membership club from the dashboard.

#### Scenario: Loyalty dashboard makes referrals first-class
- WHEN an owner/admin opens the loyalty dashboard
- THEN the dashboard SHALL show bring-a-friend visibility alongside the rest of the loyalty program
- AND it SHALL surface referral advocates, referred guests, and referral-ready rewards without requiring the owner to inspect raw reward rows only

#### Scenario: Owner reviews loyalty health in one place
- WHEN an owner/admin reviews the loyalty dashboard
- THEN the page SHALL combine member performance, referral performance, reward availability, and club messaging health in one operational view
- SO THAT loyalty does not feel buried across settings and guest records
