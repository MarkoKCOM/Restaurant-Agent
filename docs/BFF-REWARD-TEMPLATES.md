# BFF Reward Templates

Use this when the owner asks "what should I offer?".

The rule is simple:
one sharp offer for a clear moment beats a messy catalog full of random discounts.

## What a good BFF reward looks like

- easy to explain in one line
- easy for staff to honor without confusion
- feels generous to the guest
- protects margin
- nudges a specific behavior

## Default template library

| Template | Guest moment | Suggested reward | Why it works |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sweet comeback | returning guest, almost-ready comeback | Dessert on next visit | Feels generous, low friction, usually margin-friendly |
| Midweek mover | soft Sun-Wed demand, lapsed guest | 10% off midweek | Fills weak nights without discounting peak nights |
| Table starter | social booking, referral, casual group | Starter for the table | Encourages more covers and is easy to talk about |
| Birthday table treat | birthday, anniversary, celebration | Small table treat / dessert platter | Creates a memorable hospitality moment |
| Bring-a-friend | referral | Dessert or starter when a new friend joins | Clear value and easy word-of-mouth |
| Host perk | 6+ party, organizer guest | Perk for the host | Gives one person a reason to gather everyone at BFF |

## What to avoid

- generic cash discounts with no timing or purpose
- too many offers running at once
- rewards staff can’t verify fast
- anything that sounds like bribing for a review
- offers that are stronger than needed for a loyal regular

## Mapping moments to offers

### Returning guest
Best first move:
- dessert on next visit

### Lapsed guest
Best first move:
- 10% off midweek

### Birthday or anniversary
Best first move:
- celebration treat for the table

### Referral
Best first move:
- starter or dessert linked to bringing a friend

### Group organizer
Best first move:
- host perk on 6+ covers

## Agent rule

Jake should not throw the whole catalog at the guest.
He should pick the 1-2 offers that actually fit the guest’s situation and say them like a human.

Good:
- "יש לי משהו קטן ששווה לך לביקור הבא"
- "אם תבוא באמצע שבוע, שמרתי לך 10%"
- "על קבוצה כזאת יש לי צ׳ופר למארח"

Bad:
- long bullet list of every reward in the system
- hard-sell coupon language
- formal marketing copy
