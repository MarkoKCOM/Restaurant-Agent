07 — Usage

Do & don't

✓ Do

  • Use on paper, white, or ink backgrounds.
  • Use the reverse mark on red brand panels.
  • Use the mono variant for single-colour print.
  • Keep ¼-height clear space around the mark.
  • Use the SVG favicon for crisp rendering.

✕ Don't

  • Don't recolour the chair to non-brand colours.
  • Don't stretch, skew, or rotate the mark.
  • Don't add shadows, bevels, or gradients.
  • Don't place the primary mark on busy photos.
  • Don't shrink the wordmark below 88px wide.
