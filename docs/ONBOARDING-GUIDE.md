# OpenSeat — Restaurant Onboarding Guide
## Everything You Need to Get Started

---

## Overview

OpenSeat connects your restaurant to customers through WhatsApp. Your customers message your restaurant's WhatsApp number and our AI bot handles reservations, membership club flows, rewards, and retention — automatically, in Hebrew.

**Setup time:** ~30 minutes
**What you need:** A dedicated phone number + basic restaurant info

---

### Option C: Add a Line to Your Existing Plan

Call your carrier and ask to add a secondary line (קו נוסף). This is often the easiest option — they'll mail you a SIM or activate an eSIM. Usually ₪20-30/month extra.

### Important Notes
- ⚠️ The number must be able to **receive SMS** (for WhatsApp verification)
- ⚠️ Do NOT use a number that's already on WhatsApp — it will disconnect the existing account
- ✅ After initial setup, you don't need to keep the SIM in a phone — we handle everything from the server

## Step 2: Register the Number on WhatsApp

Once you have the SIM:

1. Put the SIM in any phone (temporary — just for setup)
2. Install WhatsApp if not already installed
3. Register the new number on WhatsApp
4. Verify via SMS code
5. **That's it** — after verification, we take over from our server. You can remove the SIM from the phone.

We'll handle the rest: connecting the number to our system, setting up the bot profile picture, status, and business info.

---

## Step 3: Prepare Your Restaurant Info

Send us the following (WhatsApp message, email, or however you prefer):

### Required
- [ ] **Restaurant name** (as you want it to appear)
- [ ] **Address**
- [ ] **Opening hours** (for each day of the week)
- [ ] **The new WhatsApp number** from Step 1
- [ ] **Owner's personal WhatsApp** (for notifications and daily reports)
- [ ] **Logo** (for the bot's WhatsApp profile picture)

### Nice to Have (Can Add Later)
- [ ] **Menu** (PDF, photo, or link — for the bot to answer food questions)
- [ ] **Instagram / Facebook links** (bot can share these)
- [ ] **Google Maps link** (bot sends this when asked for directions)
- [ ] **Cancellation policy** (if any)
- [ ] **Regular events** (quiz nights, happy hour, live music — bot can promote these)

## Step 4: Set Up Your Rewards

Decide what customers can earn. Here's what comes ready out of the box:

### Stamp Card (כרטיס חותמות)
- Customer gets a stamp per visit
- After **10 stamps** → free reward (you choose what)
- Example: "10 ביקורים = בירה עלינו"

### Points System (נקודות)
Every visit earns points. Customers redeem points for rewards you define:

| Example Reward | Points Cost |
|---------------|-------------|
| 🍺 Free beer | 50 |
| 🍟 Free appetizer | 80 |
| 🥩 Main dish upgrade | 150 |
| 🎉 Free dessert | 100 |

**You decide the rewards and prices.** We'll set them up.

### Tiers (דרגות)
Automatic — customers level up based on visits:

| Tier | After X Visits | Points Per Visit |
|------|---------------|-----------------|
| 🥉 Bronze | 0 | 10 |
| 🥈 Silver | 5 | 15 |
| 🥇 Gold | 15 | 20 |

### Referrals (הפניות)
Each customer gets a unique referral code. When their friend visits:
- Referrer gets **50 points**
- New customer gets **25 points**

## Step 5: Go Live

Once we have your info and number:

1. We connect the WhatsApp number to our system (~10 minutes)
2. We configure the bot with your restaurant's details, hours, and rewards
3. We send you a test message to verify everything works
4. **You're live!**

### How to Tell Customers

- Put a small sign at the bar/tables: **"שלחו לנו הודעה בוואטסאפ! [number]"**
- Add the number to your Instagram bio
- Tell regulars in person: "יש לנו מועדון לקוחות חדש בוואטסאפ"
- The bot handles the rest — after first contact, it can enroll them into your membership club flow

## FAQ

**How much does it cost?**
Pilot launch pricing starts at ₪299/month for the first 5 restaurants. Standard plans start at ₪499/month depending on restaurant size.

**Do I need to do anything day-to-day?**
No. The bot runs automatically. You'll get a daily summary and can check the dashboard anytime. The only manual action is marking reward redemptions when a customer shows a code.

**Can I see what the bot is saying to customers?**
Yes — everything is visible in the dashboard under guest conversations.

**What if a customer asks something the bot can't handle?**
The bot will let them know a human will follow up, and you'll get a notification.

**Can I change rewards later?**
Yes, anytime — from the dashboard or just message us.

**Is this WhatsApp Business API?**
No — we use a direct WhatsApp connection that's simpler and doesn't require Meta Business verification. Your customers message a regular WhatsApp number.
